Fishing

As a kid I learned to fish with my grandfather and my dad. They both started taking my on camping and fishing trips when I was as young as 8 years old, and it is still a relaxing hobby that I enjoy today.



Frisbee

Frisbee was something I learned that I enjoyed when I was 7 years old. My third grade teacher would bring her frisbee catching dogs to recess and taught up how to properly throw a frisbee. Ever since then I enjoyed frisbee, and even played on my Ultimate Frisbee Team in college.
