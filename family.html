Parents

My parents are some of the best you could ask for. We were lucky enough that my mom got to stay home with us most of our childhood. My dad worked a normal 9-5 job and was home most weekends. Both of my parents stayed very involved in my life through highschool, and while they still live in Atlanta I keep up with them weekly.

Brother

My brother and I are 4 years apart. In the 90's we did not get along, as he was always copying me and driving me crazy. However as we grew up by the time I was in college and he was in highschool, he had become my best friend. He later went on to be the best man in my wedding.
